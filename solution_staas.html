Storage as a Service
(STaaS)

Supports automatic storage service provisioning, intelligent data management, and moving mission-critical services to the cloud, accelerating digital transformation at data centers.

INTERACTIVE Demo

STaaS solution overview

  • Various storage services Automatically allocates storage resources and achieves minute-level TTM.
  • On- and off-premises data collaboration Enables on-demand data flow and reduces TCO by 20%.
  • Intelligent maintenance and prediction Flexibly changes SLA and improves efficiency by 50%.
  • Unified multi-device management Simplifies multi-vendor device management and improves efficiency by 50%.

Advantages

  • Rich Services
    Supports multiple cloud platforms Delivers a variety of storage and data protection services for various cloud platforms, such as Huawei FusionCloud private cloud, vRealize private cloud, and other third-party cloud platforms. Supports multiple features Provides common storage services (e.g., blocks, files, and objects) and data protection services such as snapshots, replication, active-active, and backup.
  • Easy Management
    Self-help services Automatically provides storage resources and enables minute-level TTM based on service catalogs and SLAs to quickly respond to business needs.
          Unified service and O&M interfaces Offers a unified interface for managing storage services and service-related O&M monitoring (alarms, capacity, and performance) without frequent switchovers, greatly simplifying operations.
  • Intelligent Data
    Intelligent prediction Monitors storage service quality based on service objectives; provides performance and capacity statistics reports, and predicts capacity trends based on such statistics to support service planning and shortens analysis time for service decisions such as capacity expansion, procurement, and migration.

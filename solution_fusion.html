FusionROBO

An integrated solution designed for Remote Offices/Branch Offices (ROBOs) of large enterprises. It integrates all IT resources such as servers, storage devices, switches, firewalls, routers, and UPSs — all in a single cabinet.

Remotely manages computing, network, and storage devices and resources of 500 branch sites, reducing O&M travel expense by 90% and O& M labor costs by 30%.

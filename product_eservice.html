Product Advantages

  • Cost Effectiveness
    Supports data-driven IT organization transformation.
    • Monitors and predicts capacity usage, effectively predicting data requirements to provide capacity expansion and recycling suggestions.
    • Intelligent disk fault prediction improves data asset security.
    • Provides configuration optimization suggestions based on the performance regularity.
  • Efficiency Improvement
    Exposes risk factors based on historical data to avoid problems.
    • Automatically monitors data with 24/7 hours, improving O&M efficiency
    • Automatically reports faults and creates work orders to reduce support costs.
    • Automatically predicts performance capacity with an accuracy rate of 82%.
  • Data Security
    Exposes risk factors based on historical data to avoid problems.
    • All operations performed on the eService client are recorded, and integrity check is performed on data to prevent data tampering.
    • Bidirectional authentication is implemented between eService and Huawei clouds.
    • Information security complies with the ISO/IEC 27001:2013 Standard. Data is stored and encrypted using AES 256.

Capacity Plan

Performance Forecasting
Predicts performance in the next 60 days to identify performance risks.
  • 100,000+ storage devices, 20,000,000,000+ feature data, and 1,000+ scenarios, providing data sources for performance analysis

Intelligently Identifying Risky Disks
Identifies disk risks 14 days in advance to avoid service impact.
  • Analyzes 200,000+ disks and 20,000,000,000+ pieces of feature data.

  • Verifies models from enterprise data centers for more than 300 days to identify disk risks 14 days in advance.

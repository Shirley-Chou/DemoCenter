Snapshot backup in seconds,
30x backup frequency improvement

Leverages the snapshot technology of storage devices, achieves a short backup time with zero impact on production services and high-frequency backups.

Incremental recovery,
20x recovery acceleration

Uses the snapshot's incremental recovery technology, compares the backup copy with the production data. Only the change data is recovered and the recovery time is shortened.

On-demand protection
based on the data lifecycle
User-defined backup policies:

More high-frequency copies are retained in the recent data, and a small number of copies are retained in the long-term data.

Intelligent distribution of backup copies:

The production site saves the copies of recent data that is accessed frequently, and the DR site saves the copies of other data.

Automatic snapshot deletion:

The system automatically combines and deletes early copies based on the retention policy to release storage space.
